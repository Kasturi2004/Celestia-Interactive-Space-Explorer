COSMIC CONTEXT

A story written in starlight.

Our solar system is an evolving archive of the universe. Its worlds preserve evidence from its very first days.

01

Formation

4.6 billion years ago, a cloud of gas and dust collapsed under gravity, igniting our Sun and forming a spinning protoplanetary disk.

02

Planet families

Four rocky terrestrial planets orbit close to the Sun; beyond them, giant gas and ice worlds hold most of the system's mass.

03

Asteroid Belt

Between Mars and Jupiter lies a broad band of ancient rocky debris—material that never became a planet.

04

Kuiper Belt

Past Neptune, an icy frontier surrounds the solar system, home to dwarf planets, comets, and primordial remnants.
